# Digital Dugnad 2026

Recently I got a mail from about Digital Dugnad: How can a dugnad save the web?

## So what is a Dugnad?

Dugnad is a Norwegian word and tradition: It's about coming together when a task feels too big to do alone.

Link: [digitaldugnad.eu](https://digitaldugnad.eu/en/)

Well let's have a look at what (European?) options we have.

## 1. Switch your browser.

If you would stop using **Google Chrome** and **Microsoft Edge** what then?

- Ecosia = European (Berlin, Germany) [www.ecosia.org](https://www.ecosia.org/)
- Vivaldi = European (Norway and Iceland) [www.vivaldi.com](https://vivaldi.com/)
- Zen Browser = [zen-browser.app](https://zen-browser.app/)
- Firefox = Mozilla Foundation, Non-profit based in California, USA [www.firefox.com](https://www.firefox.com/)
- Ladybird = all new browser engine, still in active development [www.ladybird.org](https://ladybird.org/)

## 2. Switch your search engine.

If you would stop using **Google Search** and **Microsoft Bing** what then?

- Ecosia = European (Berlin, Germany) [www.ecosia.org](https://www.ecosia.org/)
- Qwant = European (Paris, France) [www.qwant.com](https://www.qwant.com)
- DuckDuckGo = American company focused on privacy

## 3. Move your email.

If you would stop using **Google Gmail** and **Microsoft Hotmail / Outlook / Live** what then?

- Proton Mail = European (Switserland) [www.proton.me](https://proton.me/)

There are more options, will add later...
