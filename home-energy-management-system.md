# Build your Home Energy Management System (with Victron)

On this page I'm going to explain how to build your own Home Energy Management System.

## Victron Energy?

Victron Energy is a Dutch company with roots in the marine industry, known for its robust, off-grid capable energy solutions.

Many ships, campers, ambulances and houses have Victron Energy systems.

Link: [www.victronenergy.com](https://www.victronenergy.com/)

## Why Victron?

Their systems are designed for independence:

- European company
- No subscription fees
- No lock-in to energy suppliers
- Off-grid capable
- Programmable
- Expand as needed

Whether you're looking to integrate solar panels, batteries, EV charging, or hot water systems, Victron provides the tools to create a tailored, future-proof energy system for your home.

## Do I need off-grid capability?

Is your power grid really good and there are very few outages? Good for you!

But, turn off your power and see what your life looks like!

Try to live without power for 24 hours and see how dependant we have become!

Think about your food in the fridge and the freezer.
