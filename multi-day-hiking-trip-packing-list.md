# Multi-Day Hiking Trip Packing List

This packing list is aimed towards multi-day hiking trips when walking from one camping to another.

## Pierpad from Venlo to Sittard

Last year (June 2025) I did my first multi-day hike in The Netherlands and walked 4 days on the ["Pieterpad"](https://pieterpad.nl/)

I took the bus to Venlo trainstation and started walking south. Then I walked from camping to camping, I booked the next camping during the day. 

## Upcoming hiking trips 2026

**July 2026:** Mullerthal Trail, Luxemburg

## Campings

A camping provides:

- Camping spot
- Toilet
- Shower
- Water refill
- Recharge phone + powerbank

Some campings had a restaurant nearby or I warmed my own meal.

## What did I bring?

**Backpack 38 Liter**

This backpack was just big enough: QUECHUA MH500 38 liter (Decathlon)

## Walking stuff

- Water (camelbag 2 Liter)
- Cap
- Sunscreen
- Smartphone (for navigation)
- Powerbank

## Sleeping stuff

- Tent
- Sleeping mat
- Sleeping bag
- Pillow
- Earplugs
- Headlamp
- Pump

## Showering stuff

Separate bag:

- Short (small short)
- Slippers
- Toilet paper (little bit)
- Towel
- Shower gel
- Toothbrush
- Toothpaste
- Charging cable (charge phone / powerbank while showering)
- Deodorant

## Eating & Drinking

- Billycan with Gas and Burner and stand inside
- Fold-A-Cup
- Coffee drip system
- Military ration pack
